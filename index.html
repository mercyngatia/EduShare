Stakeholder Outcomes

Our impact measurement follows the DeCAL methodology (Define, Collect, Analyze, Leverage) to ensure accurate reporting. By comparing pre- and post-intervention data, we can demonstrate the tangible impact of our programs:

EduShare 2024 Impact Report

This report demonstrates the impact of our educational resource sharing platform based on data collected through our system.

Executive Summary

In 2024, EduShare facilitated the connection between donors and educational organizations across 45 countries, impacting over 150,000 students through resource sharing.

Key Metrics

Resources Distributed: 125,482

Books, supplies, and equipment shared with educational organizations

Schools Supported: 2,417

Educational institutions receiving resources through our platform

Community Impact: 78%

Of recipient schools reported improved educational outcomes

Carbon Footprint Reduced: 125T

Tons of CO2 saved through resource sharing and waste reduction

Monthly Resource Distribution

Stakeholder Outcomes

Our impact measurement follows the DeCAL methodology (Define, Collect, Analyze, Leverage) to ensure accurate reporting.

  • 94% of donors report high satisfaction with the donation process
  • 87% of recipients indicate resources received were exactly what they needed
  • 62% reduction in educational resource waste in participating communities

Geographical Impact

Environmental Benefits

By facilitating the reuse of educational resources, EduShare has contributed significantly to environmental sustainability:

  • 85 tons of educational materials diverted from landfills
  • 12,000 lbs of CO2 emissions avoided
  • Conserved resources equivalent to 5,000 trees

Case Studies

Greenfield Elementary School

After receiving 200 books and 15 tablets through EduShare, Greenfield Elementary saw a 25% improvement in reading test scores and a 40% increase in student engagement in STEM activities.

Community Learning Center

The Community Learning Center in Detroit received art supplies for their after-school program, allowing them to expand from 30 to 75 students and provide a creative outlet for underserved youth.

Future Goals

In 2025, we aim to expand our reach to 60 countries and impact 250,000 students through our platform. We're committed to continuously improving our impact measurement and reporting processes.
